Hematology Regulatory Landscape

Hematology regulation involves FDA's guidance on CAR-T cell therapy development, EMA advanced therapy medicinal product (ATMP) requirements, and evolving gene therapy long-term follow-up mandates.

Regulatory Requirements

Key Regulatory Frameworks for Hematology

Comprehensive coverage of FDA, EMA, and PMDA requirements specific to hematology drug development and benefit-risk assessment.

FDA

  • ✓ Cell and gene therapy guidance (CBER)
  • ✓ CAR-T REMS requirements
  • ✓ long-term follow-up for gene therapy (15 years)
  • ✓ breakthrough therapy for hematologic malignancies

EMA

  • ✓ ATMP regulation (Regulation 1394/2007)
  • ✓ CAR-T hospital exemption considerations
  • ✓ gene therapy long-term efficacy and safety follow-up
  • ✓ CHMP/CAT joint assessment

PMDA

  • ✓ Japan regenerative medicine framework (SAKIGAKE for cell therapies)
  • ✓ conditional time-limited approval for ATMPs
  • ✓ PMDA gene therapy safety consultation
