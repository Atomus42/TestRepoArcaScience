Case Study 1 Pediatric Epilepsy (Ages 2-12)

Anti-Epileptic Drug

Challenge

Assessing seizure control benefit against cognitive development and growth effects in school-age children requiring long-term treatment

ArcaScience Approach

Integrated neurodevelopmental outcomes from clinical trials with growth trajectory analysis and cognitive assessment data across 1,800 pediatric patients

Results

  • ✓Growth impact quantified by age group
  • ✓Cognitive safety signal detection automated
  • ✓PIP-compliant BRA documentation prepared
Case Study 2 Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis

Biologic (Anti-TNF)

Challenge

Long-term immunosuppression safety in developing immune systems with limited pediatric-specific safety data

ArcaScience Approach

Pediatric safety extrapolation from adult RA data with age-stratified infection risk modeling and vaccination response monitoring

Results

  • ✓Pediatric infection risk contextualized
  • ✓Vaccination response impact characterized
  • ✓PREA post-marketing study design supported
Case Study 3 ADHD (Ages 6-17)

ADHD Stimulant Medication

Challenge

Balancing attention/behavior improvement against growth suppression, cardiovascular effects, and substance abuse potential

ArcaScience Approach

Long-term growth trajectory modeling with cardiovascular safety monitoring and behavioral outcome assessment across 3,200 pediatric patients

Results

  • ✓Growth suppression trajectory predicted by age of initiation
  • ✓Cardiovascular monitoring framework established
  • ✓Annual PSUR generation automated
