1

Anti-TSLP Monoclonal Antibody

Severe Uncontrolled Asthma

Challenge

Broad-mechanism biologic required differentiated safety profiling from established anti-IgE, anti-IL-5, and anti-IL-4R biologics

ArcaScience Approach

Built multi-biologic comparative BRA integrating data from PATHWAY, NAVIGATOR, and CASCADE trials with post-marketing data for competing biologics

Results

  • ✓Demonstrated differentiated safety profile across 5 biologic mechanisms
  • ✓Generated payer-ready BRA supporting all-comer asthma indication
  • ✓Reduced comparative dossier preparation from 4 months to 19 days
2

Triple CFTR Modulator Combination

Cystic Fibrosis (F508del)

Challenge

Triple combination therapy required complex drug interaction safety modeling and hepatotoxicity surveillance across 3 active components

ArcaScience Approach

Developed multi-component interaction model integrating PK/PD data, hepatic safety biomarkers, and QT assessments for each component and combination

Results

  • ✓Identified hepatotoxicity interaction threshold for dose adjustment
  • ✓Modeled drug-drug interactions across 847 concomitant medication combinations
  • ✓Supported FDA and EMA simultaneous submission with unified BRA
3

Inhaled PDE4 Inhibitor

Moderate-to-Severe COPD

Challenge

Inhaled delivery route required lung deposition safety modeling distinct from oral PDE4 inhibitor safety profile

ArcaScience Approach

Separated systemic vs. local safety signals using inhaled drug deposition models and comparative analysis with oral roflumilast data

Results

  • ✓Demonstrated 78% lower GI adverse event rate vs. oral PDE4 inhibition
  • ✓Built device-drug combination BRA accepted by FDA OCP
  • ✓Predicted real-world adherence impact on benefit-risk balance
