Key Regulatory Frameworks for Immunology

Comprehensive coverage of FDA, EMA, and PMDA requirements specific to immunology drug development and benefit-risk assessment.

FDA

  • ✓ JAK inhibitor boxed warning requirements
  • ✓ REMS for serious infections/malignancies

  • ✓ biologics price competition pathway

EMA

  • ✓ Biosimilar guidelines including automatic substitution considerations
  • ✓ immunogenicity guidance for biologics
  • ✓ risk management plans for immunomodulators

PMDA

  • ✓ Japan biosimilar guidelines
  • ✓ immunogenicity testing requirements specific to Japanese population
  • ✓ post-marketing surveillance mandates for biologics
