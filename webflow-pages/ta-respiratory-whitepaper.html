Executive Summary

AI-Driven BRA for Respiratory

This whitepaper details AI-driven benefit-risk analysis for respiratory drug development, including inhaled therapies for asthma and COPD, biologics for severe asthma, and CFTR modulators for cystic fibrosis. It addresses device-drug combination product safety, inhaled drug deposition modeling, and exacerbation endpoint analysis.

Respiratory BRA requires integration of device performance data with drug safety profiles, pulmonary function endpoints (FEV1, PEF), exacerbation rates, and patient-reported outcomes. The ArcaScience platform handles the complexity of combination product assessment while maintaining regulatory compliance across FDA, EMA, and PMDA requirements.
