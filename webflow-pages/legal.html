Compliance

Our Commitments

GDPR Compliant

ArcaScience is headquartered in France and operates as an EU data controller under the General Data Protection Regulation. We have appointed a Data Protection Officer and maintain full GDPR compliance across all operations.

CCPA Compliant

For California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information and support all CCPA rights including the right to know, delete, and opt-out.

WCAG 2.1 AA

We are committed to making our website and platform accessible to everyone. Our goal is full conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA success criteria.

Questions About Our Legal Policies?

If you have questions about any of our legal documents or need to exercise your data protection rights, please contact our legal team.
