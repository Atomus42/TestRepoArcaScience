The Challenge

Scaling BRA Services Across a Diverse Multi-Sponsor Portfolio

As a leading CRO managing pharmacovigilance for 50+ sponsor companies, ICON faced unique challenges in delivering consistent, high-quality BRA across a diverse portfolio. Each sponsor had different data formats, safety database systems, and reporting requirements. Manual BRA preparation required extensive customization for each sponsor, limiting scalability.

The specific challenges facing ICON's pharmacovigilance operations were significant:

  • Consultant time allocation: ICON's PV consultants spent 65% of their time on data preparation rather than scientific analysis, limiting the value they could deliver to sponsors
  • Competitive pressure: Other CROs were investing in technology-driven differentiation, demanding faster turnaround and more advanced analytical capabilities
  • Inconsistent quality: The lack of a standardized platform meant quality and timelines varied significantly across engagements, with consistency scores ranging from 60% to 90%
  • Scalability constraints: Manual, consultant-dependent processes could not scale to meet growing demand without proportional headcount increases
  • Sponsor onboarding delays: Integrating a new sponsor's data systems and processes took 4–6 weeks, slowing revenue recognition and client satisfaction

The Solution

ArcaScience Multi-Tenant BRA Platform

ArcaScience partnered with ICON to deploy a multi-tenant BRA platform capable of serving 50+ sponsor companies simultaneously, with configurable workflows that adapt to each sponsor's specific requirements while maintaining consistent quality standards.

Implementation Process

Weeks 1–3
