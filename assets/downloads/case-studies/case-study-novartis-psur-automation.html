The Challenge

Enterprise-Scale PSUR Compliance Across a Massive Portfolio

Novartis faced systemic challenges managing PSURs for 300+ products. 22% of PSURs were submitted with deadline pressure in the final 48 hours. Manual preparation required 16–20 weeks per complex PSUR. Cross-functional coordination across 8 global safety hubs was inconsistent. The EMA's stricter timelines and format requirements increased compliance risk. Annual PV operations cost exceeded $85M with significant FTE allocation to document preparation rather than scientific analysis.

The Solution

ArcaScience Enterprise PSUR Automation Platform

ArcaScience implemented an enterprise-wide PSUR automation platform, creating standardized workflows and AI-driven document generation across Novartis's entire product portfolio.

Implementation Process

Weeks 1–4
Enterprise Assessment
Audited existing PSUR workflows across all therapeutic areas. Mapped data sources and system integrations (Argus, AERS, clinical databases). Defined standardized templates and quality criteria.
Weeks 5–10
Platform Configuration
Connected to Novartis's global safety databases. Configured AI models for each therapeutic area's signal detection needs. Built automated data extraction and aggregation pipelines.
Weeks 11–16
Pilot Deployment
Rolled out to first 50 products across 3 therapeutic areas. Validated output quality against historical PSURs. Refined AI models based on reviewer feedback.
Weeks 17–20
Enterprise Rollout
Extended to remaining 250+ products. Trained 120+ PV professionals across 8 global hubs. Integrated with existing regulatory submission systems.
Weeks 21–24
Optimization & Handover
Fine-tuned automation based on first full cycle results. Established ongoing monitoring and model update processes. Transferred operational ownership to Novartis PV team.
